The Genomics Virtual Laboratory takes the IT out of Bioinformatics. It provides a cloud-based suite of genomics analysis tools that would normally require specialist assistance. Refer to workbench services for full details.

Galaxy is an open, web-based platform for data intensive biomedical research. The Galaxy team is a part of BX at Penn State, and the Biology department at Johns Hopkins University. The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NHGRI, NSF, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins.

If you use the GVL in your research, please cite our paper:

Afgan E, Sloggett C, Goonasekera N, Makunin I, Benson D, Crowe M, Gladman S, Kowsar Y, Pheasant M, Horst R, Lonie A., Genomics Virtual Laboratory: A Practical Bioinformatics Workbench for the Cloud., PLoS One. 2015 Oct 26;10(10):e0140829.
